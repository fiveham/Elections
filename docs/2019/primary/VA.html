Districts and Candidates for the June 11th State Legislature Election in VA

Clicking on a district brings up an infowindow listing the candidates, each paired with their party affiliation, all arranged in a table. The rows of the table are sorted in alphabetical order. Because party is listed before candidate name, this also clusters candidates by party.

The names, party affiliations, and districts of the candidates come from the Virginia Department of Elections. Several districts had no candidates listed at all for some reason.

Polling Places

A registered Virginia voter can look up his or her polling place online.

An unregistered non-voter can register to vote online, in person, or by mail.
