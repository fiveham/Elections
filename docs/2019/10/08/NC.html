NC Municipal Elections, October 8th, 2019

Twenty municipalities in NC will hold elections for local offices on October 8th, 2019. In alphabetical order, these are:

  • Burlington
  • Dunn
  • Durham
  • Elizabeth City
  • Fayettevillle
  • Goldsboro
  • Henderson
  • Hickory
  • High Point
  • Mount Airy
  • Raleigh
  • Rocky Mount
  • Shelby
  • Statesville
  • Cary
  • Dobbins Heights
  • Erwin
  • Fletcher
  • Matthews
  • Mooresville
Many more will hold theirs on November 5th.

Counties are represented on this map if any part of the county is involved with any municipal election.
