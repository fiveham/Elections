Unofficial district shapes

Official shapefiles were not available for state supreme court districts or BESE districts. The districts of those two layers of this map are a best-effort approximation constructed by combining voting tabulation district (voting precinct) shapes from 2017 after using the LA Secretary of State's sample ballot lookup to identify which precincts pertain to which districts. There were naming mismatches between the SOS precincts and the precincts from the 2017 shapefile whose geometries were used to build the districts seen in this map. As such, some judgement calls were needed to assign certain precinct shapes to districts, even with a rich description of the canonical correspondence between official existing precincts and their districts.
